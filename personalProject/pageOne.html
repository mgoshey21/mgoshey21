12 Main Archetypes

There are 12 main Archetypes that are the most commonly seen in media.
They are:

  • The Hero
  • The Magician
  • The Outlaw
  • The Explorer
  • The Sage
  • The Innocent
  • The Creator
  • The Ruler
  • The Caregiver
  • The Everyman
  • The Jester
  • The Lover

The Hero

The Hero is a character who rises above a challenge and saves the day! They are usually the protagonists of their stories. Heroes are couragous, persevere through the hard trials, and have a strong sense of honor. However, they can easily fall into overconfidence and hubris.

The Magician

The Magician is a powerful figure who has found a way to harness the powers of the universe to achieve their goals. They have a strong sense of discipline, but they can be arrogant because of their power.

The Outlaw

The Outlaw is usually a rebel. They won't follow society's rules or bend to their demands. They are independent in both thinking and in action.

The Explorer

The Explorer is a character that is natuarally driven to push the boundries of the status quo. They are driven to explore the unknown. Explorers are curious, driven, and motivated by learning new things and self improvement. This also means that they can become restless, unreliable, and are never satisfied.

The Sage

The Sage is a wise figure who possesses knowledge for those who inquire after it. They are often a mother figure or a mentor. Sages have large amounts of wisdom, insight, or experience; yet, they can also be cautious, which causes them to be hesitant to join in on the action.

The Innocent

The Innocent is a morally pure character whose only intentions are good. They are usually children. Innocents are kind, sincere, and have a good sense of morality. However, these qualities can often make them vulnerable, naive, and they are rarely skilled.

The Creator

Creators are usually a motivated visionary who creates art or some other kind of structures during the narrative. They are creative, have a strong conviction, and have a large amount of willpower. However, they will usually be single-minded and lack practical skills.

The Ruler

A Ruler is a character who has legal or emotional power over others. They have status and resourses that other characters don't. However, they can easily be disliked by others and seem out of touch with most people.

The Caregiver

A Caregiver is a character who gives continual support to others and often makes sacrifices on their behalf. They are honorable, selfless, and loyal; yet, they can lack in leadership or personal ambition.

The Everyman

The Everyman is a relatable character that the audience feels like they can relate to them in their daily lives. They are grounded and relatable. They also are usually unprepared for what's to come, and they lack any special powers.

The Jester

The Jester is a character who is funny, disarming, and insightful. They are the comic relief character that can also speak important truths. Sometimes Jesters come off as obnoxious and superfical.

The Lover

The Lover is a romantic lead that is guided by their heart. They are passionate, and have a strong sense of conviction and humanism. They can also come of as naive and irrational.
